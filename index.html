Shaping the Future of Design

The Department of Design at Tezpur University is dedicated to fostering creative excellence and innovation through interdisciplinary learning. We believe in empowering students to become visionary designers who can address complex challenges with human-centered solutions.

Our approach combines traditional design principles with cutting-edge technology, preparing students for careers that bridge creativity and functionality in an ever-evolving global landscape.

Design Thinking

A methodology that emphasizes empathy, ideation, and iterative problem-solving to create innovative solutions.

Studio Culture

A collaborative environment where students learn through hands-on projects and peer feedback in dedicated creative spaces.

Interdisciplinary Learning

Cross-pollination of ideas across disciplines, enabling students to integrate diverse perspectives into their design practice.
